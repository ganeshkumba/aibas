# 🎯 Project Executive Summary
**Current Revision:** 2026-01-23
**System Status:** **GOD-MODE ACTIVE** / Forensic-Grade Ready

## I. Project Objective
To transform a fragmented, incomplete accounting codebase into a statutory-compliant AI co-pilot for Indian businesses. The system automates the transition from raw image/PDF data to audit-ready ledger entries with full cryptographic integrity.

## II. Strategic Pillars
1. **Multimodal Vision:** Leveraging Gemini 1.5 Flash to replace standard OCR, enabling spatial reasoning on messy bills.
2. **Forensic Integrity:** SHA-256 hash-chained audit logs and metadata-based anomaly detection.
3. **Statutory Automation:** Native enforcement of MSME (Sec 43B), TDS (Sec 194), and GST POS rules.
4. **Group Visibility:** Intercompany Control Tower for multi-branch organization management.

## III. Major Milestones Achieved
- ✅ **Statutory Engine:** Implemented MSME deadlines and TDS auto-splitting.
- ✅ **Forensic Shield:** Metadata/IP anomaly detection and hash-chained audit trails.
- ✅ **Amortization Engine:** Automated multi-period expense recognition.
- ✅ **Performance Indexing:** Optimized database for high-volume financial data.
- ✅ **UX/UI:** Audit Dark Mode with real-time Security & Performance reports.

## IV. Current Readiness
The system is ready for **Full Professional Audit** and Beta Testing with group entities. High-volume throughput requires an upgrade to the Gemini "Pay-as-you-go" tier.
